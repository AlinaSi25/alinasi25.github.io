Read some information about Alina:

Alina Šigajeva is Contemporary Communication student at LCC International University in Klaipeda, Lithuania. Photography is a hobby that lets Alina express herself and gives a feeling of freedom. Romantic atmosphere in pictures inspires Alina. As you can notice in this portfolio, she loves nature, sunsets and beautiful views. While studying in LCC International University, she developed herself as a creative individual, met new people and found her beloved hobby- photography. This website is made for you to learn more about Alina and her hobby.
